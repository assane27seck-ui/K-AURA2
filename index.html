Our Story

Born from Senegal, Made for the World

K-AURA was created by Khoudia Diop to celebrate the warmth, beauty and soul of Senegal. Each candle is poured by hand, blending premium wax, carefully selected fragrance oils and thoughtful details that honor West African heritage.

Every flame is an experience: soft light, deep scent, and a moment of calm in a busy world. K-AURA invites you to slow down, breathe, and surround yourself with quiet luxury.
